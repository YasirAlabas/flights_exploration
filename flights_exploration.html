What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There are 114135 flights in the dataframe, with different seat counts for each flight and different classes, the classes were not explained in the website that the data was extracted from, hwoever, I did some digging and was able to infer the meaning according to cheapflights.ca for each class abbreviation:

F: Full First Class

P: Discounted First Class

L: Business

G: Economy
